Steve Jobs

Apple, Pixar and NeXT founder

Timeline of Steve Jobs

The events in Steve Jobs's extraordinary life.

  • 24 Feb 1955 Steven Paul was born in San Francisco, the son of Abdulfattah Jandali and Joanne Schieble. He is quickly adopted by Paul and Clara Jobs
  • 1 Apr 1976 Apple Computer Inc. is incorporated by Steve Jobs, Steve Wozniak and Ron Wayne
  • 17 Apr 1977 West Coast Computer Faire Apple makes a huge sensation at the West Coast Computer Faire with a prototype Apple II
  • Feb 1982 Rise to Fame A portrait of Steve Jobs ends up on the cover of Time Magazine, under the title 'Striking it Rich'.
  • 24 Jan 1984 "Hello, I am Macintosh" Macintosh is launched in great fanfare at Apple's annual shareholder meeting
  • 12 Oct 1988 NeXT Cube Introduction Steve Jobs introduces the NeXT Cube in San Francisco to great critical acclaim, pitching it as a workstation for higher education
  • 18 Mar 1991 Wedding Steve marries Laurene Powell in Yosemite under the blessing of Steve's old zen guru Kobin Chino. Laurene is already pregnant
  • 29 Nov 1995 Pixar IPO One week after Toy Story is out, Pixar goes public. Steve Jobs's worth rises to $1.5 billion, more than it ever was during his first tenure at Apple
  • 6 May 1998 Say hello to iMac Steve Jobs introduces Apple's revolutionary iMac at the Flint Center auditorium in Cupertino
  • 5 Jan 2000 Steve Jobs becomes Apple CEO At Macworld San Francisco, Steve Jobs drops the 'interim' in his title and officially becomes Apple's CEO.
  • 12 Jun 2005 Stay Hungry, Stay Foolish Steve Jobs makes a memorable commencement speech at Stanford University.
  • 24 Jan 2006 Disney buys Pixar The Walt Disney Company acquires Pixar for $7.4 billion.
  • 9 Jan 2007 Welcome to iPhone In his most memorable keynote presentation ever, at Macworld 2007, Steve Jobs introduces iPhone and its revolutionary touch-screen interface.
  • 27 Jan 2010 iPad Introduction After months of wild rumors, Steve Jobs unveils iPad, 'the biggest thing Apple's ever done'.
  • 5 Oct 2011 Steve Jobs dies at home, surrounded by his family
